About Us

Fitpro club 360 gym

Fitpro Club 360 Gym is one of Coimbatore's most trusted fitness establishments, conveniently located on the 3rd Floor of the Nexa Car Showroom at Thulasidamu Arcade, Sathy Road, Ganapathy. With an outstanding rating of 4.7 out of 5 from over 100 satisfied members, we have built a strong reputation for delivering real results through dedication, discipline, and expert fitness guidance. Our state-of-the-art facility is designed to cater to individuals of all fitness levels — whether you are a beginner taking your first step or an athlete pushing your limits.

At Fitpro Club 360, we believe fitness is not just a routine — it's a lifestyle. Our gym is open six days a week from 5:30 AM to 9:30 PM and on Sundays from 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM, ensuring you can work out at a time that fits your schedule. From strength training and cardio to personalised fitness plans, our experienced trainers are committed to helping you achieve your health and wellness goals in a motivating and supportive environment. Join our growing community and take the first step toward a healthier, stronger you.
